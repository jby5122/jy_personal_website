I am Jiyun Yao, you can also call me Dawn. I am an Engineer who is learning design but originally wanted to become a doctor. Yes, I guess you can tell I am an interdisciplinary engineer. I was born and raised in the seaside city, Shenzhen, China, and graduated from the Pennsylvania State University in 2020 with the degree in Industrial Engineering. Then I went to OnePlus interned in the system user experience team for a while. Now I am studying in Integrated Design & Media in New York University.

I enjoy the merge of design and engineering, and by that I want to create fancy but functional tools that can open doors for others, for all. Stresses definitely come up a lot during this progress, so I probably find some good restaurants, play some instruments, travel, bake, swim or watch Netflix in my cozy bed. It is really nice to meeting you here, now let's have some fun!
